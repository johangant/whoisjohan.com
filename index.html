About me

Practical solutions for teams that need things to work.

I have spent most of my career in UK digital agencies, working with public sector, charity, media, education, policy and professional services teams.

I have worked as a developer, architect, project lead and team lead. That mix has taught me where projects usually get stuck: unclear ownership, fragile environments, slow releases and decisions that fall apart once the work starts.

My focus now is platform engineering, team dynamics and pragmatic AI literacy: better environments, safer releases, sensible hosting, useful automation and the habits that help teams use tools like Claude, Codex and DeepSeek responsibly before and after launch.

What I do

Where I can help.

Platform engineering

I come from a web development background, with practical experience across PHP, Drupal, WordPress, Python and Node.js. I use Docker, DDEV, cloud tooling and modern CI/CD services regularly.

I am strongest where code, hosting, environments and team workflow meet. I help make those parts simpler, more reliable and easier for teams to own.

AI-literate delivery

I help teams use AI tools as part of a disciplined software development lifecycle, from discovery and prototyping through review, testing, documentation and maintenance.

That means knowing where Claude, Codex, DeepSeek and similar tools are useful, where they are risky, and how to keep human judgement, security, maintainability and delivery ownership in the loop.

Technical leadership

I have recruited, built and managed developer teams, both remote and on-site. I have also led pitches, interviews, planning and delivery standards.

I work well between senior people, product teams and engineers. I can turn technical concerns into clear choices, without losing the detail that matters.

Technical direction and mentoring

I help teams make technical decisions they can live with: reviewing builds, choosing hosting routes, spotting delivery risks, sense-checking supplier proposals and improving engineering habits through real work.

I also help engineers build clearer judgement around AI-assisted development, so speed gains do not come at the cost of quality, context or accountability.
